Title
Table is not getting sorted by the timezones correctly.
Steps to reproduce
1. Open app and add a label as 'Vancouver Time' and add Pacific time from the dropdown below and click save.
2. Click on the Add Timezone again and add label as 'Calgary time' and add Mountain timezone from dropdown and click save.
3. Check the sort order of the table.
4. Add a label as 'Hawai Time' and add Hawai time from the dropdown below and click save.

Expected Result
At step 3 Pacific time should be displayed right at the top in a table in a first row and then Calgagy time should be in a second row.
After step 4, Hawai time should be displayed right at the top of the table. 

Actual Result
At step 3, Mountain time is displayed above pacific time.
After step 4, Hawai is displayed right at the bottom instead of showing up at the top.

Severity : High
